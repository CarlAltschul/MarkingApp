Devising (0-7 marks)
Range of credible ideas perceptively devised using designing with empathy in response to the design problem that demonstrate flexibility, originality and detailed attributes.
0
6-7 marks: Wide range of credible ideas perceptively devised using designing with empathy in response to the design problem that demonstrate flexibility in ways of responding, originality and detailed attributes
4-5 marks: Range of credible ideas purposefully devised using designing with empathy in response to the design problem that demonstrate originality and detailed attributes
2-3 marks: Range of credible ideas appropriately devised using designing with empathy in response to a design problem
1 mark: Ideas superficially devised or described in response to aspects of the design problem
Proposing and Evaluating (0-8 marks)
Critical evaluation of strengths, limitations and implications of ideas against design criteria, with discerning refinement and coherent, logical design concept proposal.
0
7-8 marks: Critical evaluation of the strengths, limitations and implications of ideas against design criteria; discerning refinement of ideas based on the evaluation and stakeholder information; coherent, logical and innovative design concept proposed by combining attributes of multiple ideas and stakeholder information in the develop phase
5-6 marks: Feasible evaluation of the strengths and limitations of ideas against design criteria; effective refinements of ideas in response to the evaluation; logical and credible design concept proposed by combining ideas and stakeholder information in the develop phase
3-4 marks: Superficial evaluation of the strengths and limitations of ideas against some design criteria; make changes to ideas related to the evaluation; simple design concept proposed by selecting ideas and stakeholder information in the develop phase
1-2 marks: Statements about ideas related to some design criteria; partial design concept proposed based on an idea
Representing (0-5 marks)
Sophisticated representation of ideas and design concept using fluent sequences of ideation and/or schematic sketching to progress understanding in the develop phase.
0
4-5 marks: Sophisticated representation of ideas and a design concept using fluent sequences of ideation and/or schematic sketching to progress understanding in the develop phase
2-3 marks: Appropriate representation of ideas and a design concept using ideation and/or schematic sketching in the develop phase to progress understanding of ideas
1 mark: Cursory representation of ideas and a design concept using unclear ideation and/or schematic sketching in the develop phase
